App at Virginia Tech

At Virginia Tech I had the assignment to design a product for our elderly loved ones to remain independent and live at home without supplemental human aid.

My team created an app that encourages the elderly to take their pills using a fun and interactive application. The physical product invention kept track of participants' pill intake and signaled the app. If pills were taken on time, the user could upgrade their character and collect coins and gems.
